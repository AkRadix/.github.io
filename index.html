Our Approach

Bringing “life” to procurement contract life cycles

AK Radix consulting is focused on procurement contracts. Contracts Management lifecycle can be easily underplayed, yet, is a tool and opportunity for realizing cost savings, better engage stakeholders, and navigate supplier relationships. Opportunities to be gained from procurement contracts is increased or is reduced, by an organizations perception. Procurement sits at the core of an organization’s actions to purchase materials, supplies and services at a cost, that is effective, reliable, and achieves value for the money.

AK Radix, sees procurement contracts management, as an opportunity to engage from a position of strength, and employs key contract management best practices in supporting its clients. We nurture these as the golden keys to focus on, to keep the flames burning in the contract life cycle. The relationship with the seller, whether a once time or ongoing, to provide the products or services, is crucial, and anticipate the buyer will deliver and fulfill their end of the bargain, and the buyer will pay invoices on time, with the intent that all parties will abide by all agreed-upon terms and conditions.

5. Tracking obligations

Post award - Tracking your obligations and the suppliers obligations can keep the wheels turning smoothly in the life of the project for which a contract was designed. Supplier performance measures should be noted against promised deliverables, KPI’s and SLA’s Our support also covers: Monitoring deliverables, Performance expectations, Performance plans

6. Contract changes or disagreement
